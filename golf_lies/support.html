Golf Lies Support

Thank you for using Golf Lies.

Golf Lies helps golfers understand how different lies affect ball flight and provides practical guidance for playing common on-course situations such as fairway slopes, rough, bunkers, recovery shots, and penalty areas.

How to Get Help

  1. Review the Instructions section inside the app.
  2. Check the Lie Glossary for definitions.
  3. Email support with a description of your issue.
  4. Include screenshots if possible.

This app works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
